Some Facts On Rajasthan are:-

  • It is the largest state of India by area with 342.239 km 2.
  • It was formed in 1949 by merging 22 royal kingdoms and principalities.
  • Its capital city is Jaipur, founded by Maharaja Jai Singh II in 1727.
  • Its official language is Hindi, and its additional languages are Rajasthani and English.
  • It has a tropical desert climate, with hot summers and cold winters.

Cultural Heritage.

Rajasthan’s cultural heritage is as diverse as it is vibrant. The state is famous for its traditional music and dance forms, such as Ghoomar and Kalbeliya. The colourful attire, exquisite jewellery, and unique handicrafts add to the state’s cultural richness. The lively fairs and festivals, like the Pushkar Camel Fair and the Desert Festival, attract tourists from across the globe.
